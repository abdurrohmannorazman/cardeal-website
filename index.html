Frequently Asked Questions

What is the booking fee and is it refundable?

Yes, it’s refundable if you decide to cancel, though an administrative deduction will apply as per the Vehicle Sales Order.

What’s the down payment?

Down payment is generally around 10% of the On-the-Road (OTR) price, in accordance with hire‑purchase regulations.

Can I cancel a booking? What’s the process?

Yes, cancellation is allowed. Submit a cancellation request to your Sales Advisor, return the original VSO and receipt, and a letter will be issued.

How long do I wait after booking?

Waiting times vary by model and variant. You should check with your Sales Advisor for the most accurate timeline.

How do I track my order and loan application?

Use the UFirst portal/app to check order and payment statuses. A bank officer will contact you regarding your loan

Can I trade in my old car?

Absolutely. Indicate “Yes” for trade‑in in the online form and your Sales Advisor will assist in valuing and arranging the trade-in
